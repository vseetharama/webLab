University Announces New Research Center for Sustainability

Oct 3, 2025 • By Editorial Team

The university has secured funding to build a new interdisciplinary research center focusing on sustainable technologies, green infrastructure, and community outreach. The center will bring together faculty and students across engineering, environmental science and policy.

Students are invited to apply for research assistant positions beginning next semester. The administration has scheduled an information session on Oct 12th in the Student Union.

Campus Club Fair Draws Record Attendance

Oct 2, 2025 • Student Affairs

The annual Club Fair showcased over 120 student organizations. New clubs included the Robotics Outreach Team and Sustainable Fashion Collective. Organizers reported the highest turnout in five years.
